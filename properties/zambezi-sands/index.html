Zambezi Sands

Explore Zambezi Sands

Explore Zimbabwes Victoria Falls region from Zambezi Sands, an all-inclusive luxury tented camp nestled on the bank of the mighty Zambezi River offering complete privacy and luxury in untouched African surroundings.

Enjoy breathtaking views of the river from your private lookout deck, or venture out of the tented camp and experience an unforgettable Africa on guided game drives and walks where thriving birdlife and game await.

FOOD & BEVERAGES

Join us for meals in the lodge dining room with an open theatre kitchen where our in-house chefs can be observed creating signature dishes that fuse the best of international cuisine with traditional Zimbabwean classics, using the freshest locally sourced and seasonal ingredients.

Relax with a drink after a long day at our lounge and bar, or shaded viewing deck to enjoy signature cocktails and a curated selection of local and international beverages.

WELLNESS

Unplug, unwind, and reconnect with your body, mind, and spirit with our carefully selected wellness sessions and experiences.
