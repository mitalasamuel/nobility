]]> Get a taste of Africa at home with a signature Nobility cocktail. Our in-house crafted Nobility Boabab Retreat recipe puts the essence of this ancient African tree front and centre with Boabab power as the key ingredient in a beautiful syrup blended with gin. 

This delicious drink not only offers a unique taste but has health benefits too. Baobab powder is considered a powerful superfood rich in fibre, vitamins and minerals, known for its antioxidant and inflammation-reducing benefits. If you’re wondering where to find this speciality ingredient, most health stores and spice shops across Southern Africa will stock it. Find it online here.

This two-part recipe includes directions for creating the syrup, followed by how to use it in the cocktail.

Syrup Ingredients

1 cup granulated sugar
3/4 cup water
1 cup sliced fresh ginger root, peeled
1 tablespoon of Baobab powder

Steps

  1. Place sugar and water into a small pot on medium heat.
  2. Stir until all sugar has melted. 
  3. Add ginger and Baobab powder. Bring to a boil. 
  4. Cover, reduce heat and simmer for 15 min.
  5. Remove from the heat and steep for an hour, or until it reaches your preferred taste.
  6. Strain and bottle.

Cocktail Ingredients

50ml Victoria Falls Gin
25ml Homemade baobab and ginger syrup 
25ml Lemon juice
1 tablespoon Cucumber purée (blended cucumber)

Steps

  1. Add all liquid ingredients into a cocktail shaker filled with ice and shake.
  2. Wrap thin cucumber slices around the inside of the glass.
  3. Fill with ice.
  4. Pour into your glass of choice.

]]> At Batoka, we put people first, so when we choose who we work with, we make sure every aspect of the business is about uplifting Africa and its people. This is why we have partnered with R&R Luxury as the exclusive supplier of Batoka’s in-room care products and spa treatments.

R&R is an entirely natural, plant-based skincare brand from Africa, founded on the philosophy of transforming one of nature’s purest and richest gifts – Shea Butter – into elegant beauty products that moisturise and renew the skin.

The brand empowers hundreds of Ghanaian women in rural communities through its supply chain

Nobility seamlessly aligns with the core of R&R as in addition to crafting a luxury product, they also support and uplift local people in the process. The brand empowers hundreds of Ghanaian women in rural communities through its supply chain, all the way from the growing and harvesting of the shea crops, to the manufacturing process.

Founded by Beauty Entrepreneur Valerie Obaze in 2010, R&R has since garnered international recognition in the world of beauty and has won several awards for the quality of its range of clean beauty products. Guided by the core values of ethical sourcing, nature preservation and job creation, R&R Luxury is one of the very few companies to source, manufacture, sell and export in Africa, creating employment for hundreds of Africans, across the entire value chain in a region with high unemployment rates and a booming youth population.

Because of their commitment to using the purest natural ingredients and a devotion to excellence in quality, the R&R product’s gentle formulation makes it ideal for both men and women, as well as children and babies.
